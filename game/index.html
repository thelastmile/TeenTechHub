Welcome to Teen Tech Hub's gaming app!

Your objective is to save Princess Riley from the evil sorceress.

To save the princess, you will have to learn how to program in javascript.

Each scene contains hidden clues that will help you complete a javascript challenge.

As you progress through the game, you will be awarded points that can be redeemed at any Teen Tech Hub for prizes. The more points you gather the greater the prize.

Good luck on your quest!
