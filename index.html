Teen Tech Hub participants will be divided into 2 groups of 15 each. Groups will meet separately twice a week. On Saturdays, groups will meet and receive instructions from mentors covering: coding, design, and social media marketing. Each session is approximately 90 minutes long.

Each section consists of 9-months of vigourous training. Teens and tweens will exercise their minds by completing challenging coding exercises. They will learn what it means to be an effective team member and leader, while building skills for their futures.

Graduate students will be encouraged to continue the program as mentors. Assisting other students with their studies and challenges, as well as, helping to cope with the stresses of daily lives and teaching students how to be successful men and women.
