Teen Tech Hub participants will be divided into 2 groups of 15 each. Groups will meet separately twice a week. On Saturdays, groups meet together receive instructions and mentoring in coding, design, and social media marketing. Each session is approximately 90 minutes.

Each section consists of 9-months of vigourous training. Teens and tweens will exercise their minds through challenging coding exercises. They learn what it means to be an effective team member and leader. While building skills for their futures, in tech or elsewhere.

Graduate students will be encouraged to continue the program as mentors. Helping other students balance their daily lives and learning to be successful men and women.
