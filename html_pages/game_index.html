Welcome to Riley's Adventures in Coding!

Your objective is to guide Riley through her training, gathering clues along the way. Check objects for clues by hitting the 5 key.

Each clue contains information that you will need in order to solve a Javascript problem that appears after all the clues have been gathered.

How to Play:

Left arrow Move left
Right arrow Move right
Up arrow Move up
Down arrow Move down
9 key Jump right
7 key Jump Left
5 key Check for clues
