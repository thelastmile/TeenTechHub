Welcome to Teen Tech Hub's gaming app!

Your objective is to guide Riley through her training, gathering clues along the way.

Each clue contains information that you will need in order to solve a Javascript problem that appears after all the clues have been gathered.

Your points are redeemable for prizes at Teen Tech Hub.

How to Play:

<- (left arrow) Move left
-> (right arrow) Move right
5 key Check for clues
